Whats S&P 500 ?

  • S&P 500 is a market-capitalization-weighted index of the 500 largest U.S. publicly traded companies by market value
  • Its the most common benchmarks for the broader U.S. equity markets
  • Other common U.S. stock market benchmarks include the Dow Jones Industrial Average or Dow 30 and the Russell 2000 Index, which represents the small-cap index
